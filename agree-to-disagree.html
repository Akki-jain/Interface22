AGREE TO DISAGREE


DESCRIPTION
Debate encourages the growth of several crucial interpersonal skills, including critical thinking, conflict resolution and a larger perspective on the world. A firm grasp of the facts and the concerns and the capacity to connect them logically and persuasively is necessary for effective debating. The winner will be the one who best delivers their argument.


Duration : 1 Hour
No. of Rounds : 1
No. of Participants in a Team : 2


RULES

  • Topic will be given on-the-spot .
  • In a team, participants speaking for and against the motion would be decided by a toss.
  • Preparation time will be 2 minutes.
  • Speaking time of 7 mins followed by a 3-minute rebuttal round.
  • Usage of unparliamentary language leads to disqualification.




Interface is an inter-collegiate technical fest held annually for over 25 years by the Department of Computer Science, CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bangalore. Helmed by students of the Department of Computer Science, it aims to be at the forefront of innovation and collaboration, with new ideas and events being presented each year.
