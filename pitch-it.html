PITCH IT


DESCRIPTION
A business has only two functions - marketing and innovation. Each team will have to design the packaging for a product and then pitch it to the investors. The goal of this event is to test your marketing and on-feet thinking skills as you try to effectively market the product given to you. You will be judged based on your originality and marketing tactics.


Duration : 3 Hours
No. of Rounds : 2
No. of Participants in a Team : 4


RULES

  • The product for each team will be given 5 minutes before the event.
  • The first round is a pen and paper round. Teams will have to design a package and labeling for their product in 10 minutes followed by 3 minute presentation.
  • Winners of the first round will move to the second round.
  • In the second round, 25 minutes will be provided for creating a PowerPoint Presentation that the teams will use to pitch.
  • The teams cannot take longer than 4 minutes to pitch the product.
  • Teams can utilize online resources through out the event.




Interface is an inter-collegiate technical fest held annually for over 25 years by the Department of Computer Science, CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Bangalore. Helmed by students of the Department of Computer Science, it aims to be at the forefront of innovation and collaboration, with new ideas and events being presented each year.
